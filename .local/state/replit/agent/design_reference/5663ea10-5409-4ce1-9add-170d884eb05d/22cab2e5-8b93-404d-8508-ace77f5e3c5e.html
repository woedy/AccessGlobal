About Access Global Foundation

Founded on the belief that every community deserves access to fundamental human needs

Our Story

Access Global Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful vision: that every person, regardless of their circumstances or location, deserves access to education, healthcare, security, and basic human dignity.

Founded in 2015 by a group of passionate humanitarians, we began with a small project in rural Kenya. Today, we operate in over 25 countries, touching the lives of more than 150,000 people annually.

Our approach is rooted in community partnership, sustainable development, and the belief that lasting change comes from empowering local leaders and building local capacity.

Our Vision

A world where every community has access to the resources and opportunities needed to thrive and prosper.

Our Mission

To empower communities through sustainable programs in education, healthcare, security, and humanitarian aid.

Our Values

Integrity, compassion, sustainability, community partnership, and unwavering commitment to human dignity.
